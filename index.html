Sheet 03 · Record

Where the field grounding comes from

I spent years as a mechanical project manager before I wrote a line of AI. At Tropic Mechanical Contractors I ran the mechanical scope — estimating through closeout — on roughly $60M of healthcare and commercial construction: UHealth Doral, Baptist Health Miami Lakes, Mount Sinai Westchester, Memorial Red Road, and the Motorola Plantation offices.

When AI got good, I pointed it at the work I already knew. I built and deployed 20+ internal tools across departments — job costing, manpower tracking, schedule analysis, document management — because I understood the problems from the inside. That's the whole thesis behind FieldStrata and GNC AI Consulting.
